Welcome to Space Game

The goal of Space Game is simple: to create a fun, free, and engaging interactive mobile application that appeals to a general audience. The successes of the many great free-to-play games on the current mobile app market are an inspiration to put out a fresh new take on the classic arcade-style spaceship games of yesterday mixed with the technological capabilities of today.
